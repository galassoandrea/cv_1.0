About Me

My name is Andrea Galasso. I am a student currently completing my Master’s Degree program in Computer Engineering with a specialization in AI and Data Science at Politecnico di Bari. During my studies, I have gained strong competencies in several fields, especially artificial intelligence, working on academic projects related to recommendation systems and Graph Neural Networks (GNNs).
I also have a solid theoretical understanding of major blockchain technologies. Despite my analytical background, I am a highly creative person and I've found in web design and web development a way to express my creativity.
I'm always open to new opportunities!

During my freetime I like to do a lot of things. Music is my number one hobby. I listen to a lot of music every day, exploring always new genres and discovering always something new. I’m also a beginner guitarist and I enjoy practicing a bit every day, trying to learn always new songs and techniques.
To keep myself in good shape and health, I train at home 3 times a week balancing my workouts with strength, mobility and stretching exercises. In the weekend I usually go out for a walk. I also try to organize my meals to eat healthy most of the time.
